THE FORMAT

Two categories. Pure racing.

NON-POWERED
Pump foil
  • 1v1 Sprint Bracket — head-to-head, 200–400m closed course
  • Lap Race — multi-lap endurance on a buoy course
  • Time Trial — GPS-timed solo runs against the clock
  • Foil-only racing — no motors, no assist, no foot straps
POWERED
eFoil
  • 1v1 Sprint Bracket — short course, head-to-head finals
  • Time Trial — GPS-timed solo runs against the clock
  • Lap Race — multi-lap endurance on a buoy course
  • Open class racing — every brand on the same water, one results table
THE 1v1 FORMAT

Two courses. Never share water.

Riders race separate mirrored courses — no collision risk, clear winner every heat. Each course is symmetric: 3 turns + 180° apex + 3 turns, so neither stance is favored.

Regatta-style countdown starts. Clear winner every heat.

04
THE WEEKEND DESIGN

Everyone races. All weekend.

01

Pool play → brackets

Saturday: round-robin sprint qualifiers in pools of 4–6. Sunday: top finishers seed into main brackets, everyone else drops into a consolation bracket. Every entry guarantees water time both days.

02

Multi-format scoring

One entry covers your division's full slate — sprint bracket, lap race, and time trial. A bad sprint Saturday doesn't end your weekend; you can still chase points in lap races and time trials, and across the season.

03

Demos all weekend

Brand demo zones run continuously alongside racing. Walk-up rides, gear comparisons, family rides. The on-ramp that turns spectators and family into next year's amateur entries.

04

Sunday wraps by lunch

Finals run mid-morning, awards at 12:30, closing lunch by 1:00 PM. Riders fly home Sunday afternoon, spectators get a clean half-day, sponsors get a tight broadcast window. Respect everyone's time.

THE BUILD PATH

Four years.
Launch to world championship.

2026 is groundwork — a brand shoot, founding members, the app MVP, and Dock Bosses. The season begins in 2027 and ends Year 4 with the first FRL World Championship at Lake Tahoe.

YEAR 1 · 2027

US launch

Eight-venue US tour. Two categories. Ten divisions. Lock remaining founding members. Launch the FRL app MVP. First leaderboard champion crowned.

YEAR 2 · 2028

US scale

Ten stops. Profitability. Athlete program. Full pro qualification system live via the app. Broadcast partner negotiations.

YEAR 3 · 2029

Global

Regional licensing in Europe and Australia. App available worldwide. Continental qualifiers feed into a global leaderboard.

YEAR 4 · 2030

Worlds

Inaugural FRL World Championship at Lake Tahoe. Top qualifiers from every region. Rotates to a new global venue each year thereafter.

2026 — GROUNDWORK: Brand shoot · founding members · app MVP · Dock Bosses recruited. Building the audience before the tour rolls in.
