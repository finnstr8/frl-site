About Foil Racing League

Foil Racing League is the first US-based competitive league dedicated to flatwater foil racing. FRL competes across two categories — pump foil (non-powered) and eFoil (powered) — with eleven divisions including Pro, Open Amateur, Masters 40+, Junior U18, and Women's. Headquartered in Miami, FL. More at foilracingleague.com.
